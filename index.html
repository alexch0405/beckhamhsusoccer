About Beckham

Beckham Hsu is a 6'0" winger in the Class of 2028, a one-v-one specialist who thrives at taking defenders on off either flank. Blessed with elite, plus-plus top-end speed and the technical quality and creativity to beat his man in tight spaces, he stretches defenses and carries a constant attacking threat going at goal. He plays both wings and can operate centrally as a forward.

Beckham competes for Concorde Fire's U16 ECNL Platinum side — one of the top tiers of the Elite Clubs National League — and helped the team qualify for the 2026 ECNL National Playoffs in Del Mar, California. His development runs through Atlanta United's youth pathway: an academy tryout invitation at U11, the club's select training team at U12 and U13, and the AU Futures program during his U14 season. He has also been invited to the US Club Soccer id2 National Camp in Tampa, Florida.

A student at the Wesleyan School in Peachtree Corners, Georgia, Beckham carries a 3.5 GPA and will sit for his first ACT in fall 2026. He is pursuing a path in economics and finance, seeking a college program where he can contribute on the field while competing in a demanding classroom — committed to excellence on and off the field.
